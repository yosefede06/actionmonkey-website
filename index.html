How does it work ?

In AMNKY TOKEN we use a DAO system in which the voting power is represented by this same token with governance functions regarding specific matters of opinion not related to the Government of the Company, and is carried out automatically, managed by a smart contract.

In this way, the cumulative voting power of each user corresponds to the total amount of governance tokens accumulated in their wallet. & This voting power can be used free of charge, to intercede in any initiative proposed by the administrator through the smart contract.

All AMNKY DAO proposals will always be submitted at the initiative of Action Monkey, Permissioned DeFi Platform, the Platform itself, which allows users to vote and decide on the issues proposed by the DAO vote.

AMNKY TOKEN

AMNKY Token benefits from a system of a "Crypto staking" smart contract. Each time qualified users of the platform make a purchase or a sale, they will have to pay usage fees automatically.
